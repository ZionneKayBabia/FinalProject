What are some of the reasons behind this issue?

  • Influence From peers
    • Most people are influenced by people who they hangout with everyday. Especially their close friends.
  • Lack of Education
    • Some lack education about racial equalities.
  • Xenophobia
    • Dislike of or prejudice against people from other countries.
  • Judgemental people
    • We often put labels on people.
  • Bias
    • Bias can lead to discrimination/racism. An example is when a certain person starts to be racist because his/her idol does the same.
  • Historical Legacy of Colonialism and Slavery
    • There are histrocial inequalities that have been passed down and embedded in institutions and mindsets.
  • Stereotyping
    • Simplifies and generalizes a group of people. Oftenly in the negative light.
  • Social media
    • One of the drawbacks of social media is that it can impose standards on what people should believe. The norms regarding how to perceive an individual or a group of individuals based on their ethnicity is one of these.
